Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how T & T Temecula Cleaning Services collects, uses, and protects information you provide when you contact us, request a quote, or schedule service.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, service address, preferred scheduling details, and any notes you include about the home or requested services.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to respond to requests, provide estimates, schedule service, communicate updates, and deliver cleaning services. We may also use it for internal quality control and recordkeeping.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate our business (for example, scheduling tools, communications platforms, or payment processing), and only to provide service to you.

4. Photos and Documentation

We may take before/after photos of work areas for quality control and documentation. Photos focus on cleaned surfaces and not personal items. If you prefer no photos, notify us in writing before service.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please avoid sending highly sensitive information by email.

6. Cookies and Analytics

If we use website analytics tools, they may collect basic usage data (such as pages visited and approximate location) to help us improve the site. We do not use analytics to identify you personally.

7. Your Choices

You may request access, updates, or deletion of your contact information by emailing us. We may retain certain records for legitimate business purposes (such as completed service history).

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@temeculacleaners.com.
