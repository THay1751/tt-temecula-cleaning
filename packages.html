What’s Included?

Clear expectations. No surprises. Here are the most common questions we get in Temecula.

What’s the difference between Standard and Deep Cleaning? ⌄

Standard is a maintenance clean (surfaces, floors, bathrooms, kitchen wipe-down). Deep Cleaning adds extra detail work like baseboards (reachable), cabinet fronts, and more time on buildup areas.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment? ⌄

Yes — we bring professional supplies and equipment. If you prefer specific products, leave them out and note it in your booking form.

How do you price a 2 bed / 2 bath in Temecula? ⌄

Most 2 bed / 2 bath standard cleans land between $110–$140 depending on condition, pets, and add-ons. Deep cleans for a 2/2 typically land between $160–$240.

Are move-out cleans more expensive? ⌄

Usually yes — move-outs require more detail, edges, and inside-cabinet work. Most move-outs start at $199 and can range $180–$520+ based on home size and condition.

Do you offer small office cleaning? ⌄

Yes. Small office cleanings typically start around $120–$220 depending on square footage, restrooms, and frequency. Request a quote via the booking form.
